## What is Valgate?

Valgate is a property portfolio management platform that helps you manage your properties, tenants, work orders, compliance, and financials — all in one place.

## What you can do

- **Properties** — Add, edit, and track all your properties with detailed overviews, financials, and documents.
- **Rental Management** — Manage tenants, track leases, and monitor rent collection.
- **Work Orders** — Create and track maintenance requests from submission to completion.
- **Compliance** — Stay on top of regulatory requirements and deadlines.
- **Portfolio Analytics** — Get a bird's-eye view of your portfolio performance.
- **Estate Planning** — Organize and plan your estate documents.
