## Rent tracking

Valgate automatically tracks rent due dates based on lease terms. The rental dashboard shows:

- **Collected this month** — Total rent received
- **Outstanding** — Rent that is past due
- **Upcoming** — Rent due in the next 7 days

## Payment status

Each tenant's rent is categorized as:

| Status | Meaning |
|--------|---------|
| Paid | Full rent received for the current period |
| Pending | Payment initiated but not yet confirmed |
| Overdue | Past due date with no payment received |

## Recording manual payments

For tenants who pay offline (cash, check, bank transfer):

1. Open the tenant's rent record
2. Click **Record Payment**
3. Enter the amount, date, and payment method
4. Click **Save**

The payment is immediately reflected in the rent tracking dashboard.
