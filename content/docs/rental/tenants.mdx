## Adding a tenant

To add a tenant to a property:

1. Open the property page and navigate to the **Rental** tab
2. Click **Add Tenant**
3. Enter the tenant's contact information
4. Set the lease terms (start date, end date, monthly rent)
5. Click **Save**

## Tenant information

Each tenant record includes:

- **Contact details** — Name, phone, email
- **Lease terms** — Start/end dates, monthly rent, security deposit
- **Rent payment status** — Current, overdue, or paid
- **Documents** — Lease agreements, move-in/move-out inspection reports

## Editing a lease

To modify lease terms, open the tenant record and click **Edit Lease**. You can update:

- Rent amount
- Lease dates
- Notes and special terms

## Removing a tenant

When a tenant moves out, you can mark their lease as ended. This preserves the historical record while removing them from the active tenant list.
