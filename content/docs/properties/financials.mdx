## Financial overview

The **Financials** tab on each property page provides a snapshot of:

- **Monthly revenue** — Rent collected vs. expected
- **Annual revenue** — Year-to-date income
- **Expenses** — Maintenance, management, and other costs
- **Net operating income** — Revenue minus expenses

## Tracking income

Rent payments are automatically recorded when tenants pay through Valgate. You can also manually log payments for offline transactions.

## Tracking expenses

Work orders with vendor costs automatically contribute to expense tracking. You can also add manual expense entries for:

- Property taxes
- Insurance premiums
- Utility bills
- Management fees
- Other operational costs

## Reports

Generate financial reports for individual properties or your entire portfolio. Reports can be exported for accounting and tax purposes.
