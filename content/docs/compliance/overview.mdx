## Compliance overview

The Compliance section helps you stay on top of regulatory requirements across your portfolio. Each compliance item includes:

- **Property** — Which property the item applies to
- **Type** — Inspection, permit, certification, or other
- **Deadline** — When the requirement must be met
- **Status** — Upcoming, due, overdue, or completed
- **Documents** — Supporting files (certificates, reports)

## Adding a compliance item

1. Navigate to **Compliance** in the sidebar
2. Click **Add Compliance Item**
3. Select the property
4. Choose the compliance type
5. Set the deadline
6. Attach any relevant documents
7. Click **Save**

## Tracking deadlines

The compliance dashboard highlights items by urgency:

- **Overdue** — Past the deadline, shown in red
- **Due soon** — Within 30 days, shown in amber
- **Upcoming** — More than 30 days out, shown in green

## Completing a compliance item

When a requirement has been met (e.g., an inspection passed), open the item and click **Mark Complete**. Upload the certificate or report as supporting documentation.
