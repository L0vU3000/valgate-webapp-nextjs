## Core concepts

Valgate organizes your real estate portfolio around a few key entities:

### Properties

A **property** is the foundational unit in Valgate. Each property has its own profile page with details, financials, documents, and activity history. Properties can be residential, commercial, or mixed-use.

### Tenants

A **tenant** is associated with a property and holds lease information, contact details, and rent payment status. Tenants are managed within the Rental section of each property.

### Work Orders

A **work order** represents a maintenance request or task associated with a property. Work orders track the issue, assigned vendor, status, and completion.

### Compliance

**Compliance** items track regulatory requirements — inspections, permits, certifications — with deadlines and document attachments.

## Navigation

The main sidebar (visible on the left in the app) gives you access to:

- **Portfolio** — Overview of all properties with key metrics
- **Properties** — Browse, search, and manage individual properties
- **Work Orders** — All work orders across your portfolio
- **Compliance** — Compliance tracking and deadlines
- **Analytics** — Performance dashboards
- **Settings** — Account and notification preferences
