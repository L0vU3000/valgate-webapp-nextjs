## Step 1: Add a property

After logging in, click **Add Property** from the portfolio dashboard or the header. You'll be guided through a form to enter:

- **Property name** — A short, recognizable name (e.g., "Sunset Apartments")
- **Address** — The full street address
- **Property type** — Residential, commercial, or mixed-use
- **Units** — Number of units (for multi-unit properties)

Once saved, the property appears in your portfolio list with its own detail page.

## Step 2: Explore the property page

Click on any property to see its overview page. Here you'll find:

- Property details and photos
- Financial summary
- Active tenants and leases
- Recent work orders
- Documents

## Step 3: Add a tenant

Navigate to the **Rental** tab within a property to add a tenant. Enter their contact information, lease terms, and monthly rent amount.

## Step 4: Create a work order

From the property page or the global **Work Orders** section, create a maintenance request by describing the issue, setting a priority level, and optionally assigning a vendor.

## Step 5: Check compliance

Visit the **Compliance** section to see upcoming deadlines and add new compliance items for your properties.
