## Account settings

Access your settings by clicking **Settings** in the sidebar. From here you can manage:

- **Profile** — Your name, email, and phone number
- **Notifications** — Email and in-app notification preferences
- **Security** — Password and two-factor authentication

## Notification preferences

Valgate can send you notifications for:

- New work orders submitted
- Rent overdue alerts
- Compliance deadline reminders
- Tenant updates
- Document activity

Toggle each notification type on or off according to your preferences.

## Managing your workspace

If you manage multiple portfolios or work as part of a team, you can switch between workspaces from the header. Each workspace has its own set of properties, tenants, and settings.
